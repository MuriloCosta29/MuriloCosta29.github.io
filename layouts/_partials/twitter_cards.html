{{/* Override of Hugo's internal twitter_cards.html. Hextra calls `partial "twitter_cards.html"` from layouts/_partials/head.html. Since Hextra ships no copy of this partial, Hugo's internal one is used, which emits `twitter:card="summary"` and no `twitter:image` when no images are set.
